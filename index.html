About Sabari Group

In the early 90s, two reputed industrialists and close friends, Mr. Surinder Sharma & Dr. V. Paramesh realized that the city’s real estate industry held great potential. They also recognized that this vocation needed an emphathetic and professional touch, where care & understanding is balanced with professionalism and dedication.

This drive gave rise to the formation of Sabari Group - a construction company with strong ethics and morals, a professional outlook, and also passionate about creating quality realties. In June 1st 1990, our first project began in Chembur.

Surinder Sharma, along with second generation of the Sharma and Paramesh family, spearheads the Group’s activity in the present date
