About Sabari Group

In the early 90s, two reputed industrialists and close friends, Mr. Surinder Sharma & Dr. V. Paramesh realized that the city’s real estate industry held great potential. They also recognized that this vocation needed an emphathetic and professional touch, where care & understanding is balanced with professionalism and dedication.

This drive gave rise to the formation of Sabari Group - a construction company with strong ethics and morals, a professional outlook, and also passionate about creating quality realties. In June 1st 1990, our first project began in Chembur.

Surinder Sharma, along with second generation of the Sharma and Paramesh family, spearheads the Group’s activity in the present date

KEY FUNCTIONARIES

Mr. Surinder Sharma : A multi-faceted industrialist engaged in business, social work, the textile industry and has been associated with realty for the last 20 years.

Late Dr. V. Parmesh : A highly respected and remembered person on the team. He was a PhD in Chemical Engineering, and was in the business of chemicals. Though no more, he is always guiding us in spirit.

Ms Preeti Rajesh : A graduate from Mumbai University and holds a Post graduate Diploma in Management. She oversees accounts and financial management, along with overall administration and human resource management of the group companies

Mr. Devang Sharma : lends his expertise in real estate construction, execution, procurement and liaising. He is an MBA from University of Strathclyde, Scotland

Mr. Rajesh Natarajan : heads marketing, business development and new project acquisition for the Company. He is a qualified Construction Engineer and Management graduate from Mumbai University, and has extensive experience in marketing, business development and relationship management.

Mr. Sreedhar Venkatraman : manages the Bengaluru (Bangalore) operations of The Sabari Group. He is an engineering graduate with rich experience in manufacturing, construction management & real estate development.
